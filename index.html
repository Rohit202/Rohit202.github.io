Environment Conservation and Research Organisation (ECRO)

Environment Conservation and Research Organisation (ECRO), established on 17 August 2015 has been registered under the societies registration act, 1860 as a national NGO (Non-governmental organization) based in Goa. Since 2017, ECRO is also registered under NGO-DARPAN, a platform to promote healthy partnership between NGOs and the Government of India (GOI) under the aegis of NITI Aayog. Since its inception, ECRO has been steadily involved in many programs and interventions in the domain of Environment Conservation, primarily through research and analysis, community involvement and conservation initiatives, thereby gradually making its presence being felt throughout the state of Goa.
